MARUTIRAO MISAL












Marutirao Misalwale’s passion for food making. Started at Ahmednagar in 1983 by the food connoisseur Shri Marutirao Shinde, the legacy of the iconic dish has been carried forward by Smt. Kamalbai Shinde and her grandson Mr. Rahul Khamkar. The brand ‘Marutirao Misalwale’ was conceived with vision to offer authentic Maharashtrian delicacies made from quality ingredients and traditional food making expertise. Marutirao Misal soon became the talk of the town and was appreciated and admired by foodies of all age groups. Ridding high on its popularity quotient. Marutirao Misal was awarded ‘Assal Chav Maharahstrachi’
